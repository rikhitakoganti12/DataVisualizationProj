ABOUT THE DATASET

This dataset is related to shopping transactions made by customers in various shopping malls in hyderabad city

Each row represents a single transaction, and the dataset contains the following major columns:

  • invoice_no: An identifier for each invoice or transaction.
  • customer_id: An identifier for the customer who made the purchase.
  • gender: The gender of the customer (e.g., Male or Female).
  • age: The age of the customer.
  • category: The category of the purchased item (e.g., Clothing, Shoes, Books, Food & Beverage, Cosmetics, Toys, Technology).
  • quantity: The quantity of items purchased in that transaction.
  • price: The price of a single item in the transaction.
  • payment_method: The method of payment used for the purchase (e.g., Credit Card, Debit Card, Cash).
  • shopping_mall: The name or identifier of the shopping mall where the purchase was made.
  • invoice_date: The date of the transaction or purchase.

Using this dataset some visualizations are generated in the tableau and vizhub data visualization platforms by analyzing this dataset, retailers and shopping mall operators can gain valuable insights to optimize their inventory, marketing strategies, and overall shopping experiences for their customers. Researchers and data analysts could also use this dataset to practice data analysis and machine learning techniques.
